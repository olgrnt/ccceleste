Brian Friel

Brian Friel was an Irish playwright. Some classics include 'Philadelphia, Here I Come', 'Faith Healer', 'Dancing at Lughnasa', and 'Translations'. They usually put one of his plays on in a big theater in Dublin once every two years or so - I once saw 'Faith Healer' with Aidan Gillen in the title role - but they're well worth reading if you come across any copies.

Mainie Jellet and Evie Hone

Here are some pictures by Mainie Jellett and Evie Hone. The tall painting on the left is by Hone, as is the stained glass panel. The blue painting is by Jellett, as is the one below, titled 'Decoration' which I think I showed you before.

There was a great exhibition recently in the National Gallery of Ireland which put their work side by side - you can find the curator talking a bit about both of them here if you're interested.

Rebecca West

Rebecca West was an English journalist and writer. I mentioned her because she wrote about the Balkans in her epic piece of travel writing 'Black Lamb and Grey Falcon'.

Adam Curtis

Adam Curtis makes documentaries from achival footage for the BBC. I've only watched a couple, so I don't know if I can make any good recommendations, but most of them are available for free on this youtube channel. I think he's really good in this interview.
